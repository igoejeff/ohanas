Tripp Wilson

Singer · Songwriter · Restaurateur

The heart and soul of Ohana's — Tripp Wilson is a recording artist with original music on Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube. From the anthemic "Way Back Home" to the radio-ready "Stole My Heart", Tripp's music is the spirit of Ohana's, performed live for you tonight.

Kris Bradley

Singer · Songwriter · Producer

Acoustic Singer-Songwriter Country Soul Jazz

A Los Angeles-bred, island-adopted artist, Kris Bradley is a multi-talented singer, songwriter, and producer whose catalog spans heartfelt originals to soulful covers. Her warm, expressive voice and effortless command of the stage make every performance at Ohana's feel like a private concert under the Maui stars — intimate, powerful, and impossible to forget.

Music is how I connect. Every show, every song — it's a conversation.

Our Story

Ohana Means Family

Nestled in the heart of Kihei, Maui, Ohana's Island Bistro is more than a restaurant — it's a gathering place. We believe every evening deserves great food, great drinks, and great music shared with the people who matter most.

We are proud to be one of the only venues on Maui offering live music every single night. Whether it's a soulful solo pianist, a local band, or a surprise jam session, there's always something happening on our stage.
